About Music Reimagined

Music Reimagined started with a simple idea: music is a time machine. A single song can take you back to a first concert, a summer drive, a high school memory, or an album that never left you.

I create handcrafted record-inspired art using wood, acrylic, and laser engraving to turn those memories into something you can hang on the wall and enjoy every day.

Every piece is designed and made in Niagara Falls, New York by Celtic Grace Designs.
